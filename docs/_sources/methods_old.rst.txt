Methods
=======

Each ePSdata webpage corresponds to an ePolyScat dataset, with a unique DOI, and citation details at the foot of the page. Each webpage originates as a Jupyter Notebook, which employs `ePSproc <https://epsproc.readthedocs.io>`_ to process and visualise an `ePolyScat <http://www.chem.tamu.edu/rgroup/lucchese/>`_ dataset.
